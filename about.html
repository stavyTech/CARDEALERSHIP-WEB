About CarAutoMax

Your trusted partner in finding the perfect vehicle for your needs

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2005, CarAutoMax began as a small family-owned dealership with a simple mission: to provide quality vehicles at fair prices with exceptional customer service. What started as a modest lot with just a handful of cars has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive retailers.

Over the years, we've built our reputation on transparency, integrity, and a genuine passion for helping people find their perfect vehicle. We believe that buying a car should be an exciting, stress-free experience, and we've designed every aspect of our business with that goal in mind.

Growing With Our Community

Today, CarAutoMax offers an extensive inventory of new and pre-owned vehicles, state-of-the-art service facilities, and financing options to meet every budget. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our roots - we still treat every customer like family.

Our team of automotive experts is dedicated to staying ahead of industry trends and technologies, ensuring that we can meet the evolving needs of our customers in an increasingly digital world.
